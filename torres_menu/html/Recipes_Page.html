Pomegranate Braised Short Ribs

By Martha Stewart

Pomegranate juice and red wine compose a rich ruby-red sauce for tender beef short ribs. Garnish with jewel-like pomegranate seeds just before serving; they give tang and color and make the dish look fancy in a flash.

Daffodil Cake

By Karlynn Johnston

This cake is inspired by spring daffodils and has two gorgeous layers of fluffy and tart, with a light, airy, and bright yellow color. With a light lemon glaze and a decadent texture, this is a great cake to make for any occasion.

Mint Chocolate Brownie

By Thomas Pagot

These vegan brownies celebrate the ideal combination of chocolate and mint. A perfect, delectable summer dessert that is rich in chocolate with an amazing mint glaze.

Taste of Fall Salad

By Kristin Kossak

Fall is an opportunity to get back to salad basics: greens, great dressings, grains, and autumnal produce, so don't let the fall season end without trying this simple yet delicious salad.

Fig and Raspberry Bakewell Tart

This crowd-pleasing tart is sure to keep your family and friends entertained, with fig and raspberry in shortcrust pastry.
